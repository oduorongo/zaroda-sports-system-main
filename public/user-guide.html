Self Registration Getting Started

Configure sign-up, collect participant details, and approve records with a consistent onboarding checklist.

Registration Flow

Users create accounts, verify identity, and submit team information in one sequence. Coordinators can review pending submissions centrally and flag incomplete forms.

Coordinator Process

1

Account Creation

User signs up and validates contact details through email confirmation.

2

Team Draft Submission

Team manager adds player list, age groups, and mandatory documents.

3

Coordinator Approval

Review entries, approve valid teams, and return invalid forms with correction notes.

💡 Tip Use a standard required-fields checklist to reduce back-and-forth with managers and speed up verification.

Dashboard Overview Operations

Use dashboard widgets to manage approvals, schedule readiness, and coordinator workload in real time.

What You Can Monitor

  • Pending registrations with missing document alerts
  • Upcoming fixtures requiring venue or referee assignment
  • Recent result updates and ranking movement summaries
  • Payment status by championship and subscription tier

Widget Snapshot Table

Widget Purpose Action Trigger

Fixtures & Scheduling Module 3

Generate fixtures, assign venues, and enforce timing policies to avoid conflicts and delays.

Scheduling Playbook

1

Generate Draft Fixtures

Create automatic pairings by tier, pool rules, and available time windows.

2

Assign Venues and Officials

Allocate venue slots, match commissioners, and officiating teams.

3

Publish and Lock

Announce the final schedule and lock completed rounds to prevent accidental edits.

💡 Tip Keep a reserve slot per day for weather delays or approved match postponements.
